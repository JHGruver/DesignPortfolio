About Jacob Gruver

Designer. Technologist. Storyteller.

I'm Jacob Gruver — a UX/UI design lead with over a decade of experience designing platforms that serve the people who use them. From Boeing to AI startups, I've crafted human-centered experiences across web, mobile, AR/VR, and in-vehicle systems.

My Process

Compass-Driven Design

Every product needs a clear North Star. My method ensures we're always moving in the right direction — with empathy, strategy, and precision.

1

Context Calibration

Get to know the real world of the user. Map industry context, constraints, and mental models.

2

Compass Mapping

Build a framework through interviews, personas, and workflows that shows where we are and where we need to go.

3

Concept to Clarity

Move from rough sketches to elegant flows using wireframes, prototyping, and content strategy.

4

Refinement & Realness

Continuous testing — usability labs, heuristic reviews, dev shadowing. Don't ship until it feels right and tests right.

5

Systematize & Scale

Document everything from color tokens to screen reader labels. Make it easy to build right and grow later.
